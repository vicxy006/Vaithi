Types Of Services

Technical Work Explanation: CCTV, TV, and Wi-Fi/Fiber Installation:

CCTV installation involves mounting cameras, laying cables, connecting to DVR/NVR, setting up power, and configuring remote access. TV installation includes wall mounting, connecting HDMI and power cables, and setting up input sources. Wi-Fi and fiber installation involves running network or fiber cables, installing routers or ONTs, setting up internet configurations, and testing signal strength. All systems are tested, configured, and handed over with basic user training.

Plumbing Work Explanation:

Plumbing work involves the installation, repair, and maintenance of water supply and drainage systems. This includes laying pipes, fitting taps, sinks, toilets, and water tanks, as well as ensuring proper water flow and leak prevention. It also covers connecting appliances like geysers and washing machines, and testing all systems for leaks and pressure. The work ensures safe and efficient water distribution and waste removal in buildings.

Electrical Work Explanation:

Electrical work involves the installation, maintenance, and repair of wiring systems and electrical components. This includes laying cables, installing switches, sockets, lights, fans, circuit breakers, and distribution boards. It also involves connecting main power supply lines, testing electrical loads, ensuring safety through proper earthing, and troubleshooting any electrical faults. The work ensures safe and reliable power distribution throughout the building.
